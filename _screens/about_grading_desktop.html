Grading System Overview

A comprehensive guide to the SRM Institute of Science and Technology evaluation framework, covering internal assessments, external examinations, and grade point calculations.

Theory Courses

INTERNAL 40%

Theory assessment involves continuous internal evaluation through CTs, assignments, and surprise tests, followed by a summative semester-end examination.

Practical / Labs

Practical assessment prioritizes performance in the laboratory, records maintenance, and models, with a moderate weightage for the final practical exam.

Relative vs Absolute

SRM follows a hybrid relative grading system where the grade boundaries may shift slightly based on the class performance (mean and standard deviation) for specific batches.
